About Us

The Henry Ojogu Charity Foundation.

HOEC foundation is a registered non-governmental and non-profit organization with its address at 2b/4b State housing estate lane, Calabar Municipality Local Government area of Cross River State in Nigeria. It was founded as an organization in June 2012 and was registered in November 2014 with the corporate affairs commission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (CAC/IT/NO73606). Its main focus at the moment is in providing empowerment to young people and providing care to elderly (aged) and children of ages ranging from 5years- 16years. We have successfully carried out computer training for skills acquisition and also successful outreaches in different communities in Cross River State

MISSION STATEMENT

HOECFoundation aims to empower individuals and communities through education, health, and social support programs. We provide resources and opportunities to help vulnerable populations, fostering a compassionate environment that inspires hope and positive change.
VISION STATEMENT

We envision a world where everyone has access to the resources needed to thrive. Our goal is to build equitable and inclusive communities where education, health, and support are available to all, enabling individuals to reach their full potential.
INTEGRITY STATEMENT

HOECFoundation is committed to transparency, honesty, and ethical practices. We strive to build trust with our communities, donors, and partners by maintaining high standards of integrity and accountability.
CORE VALUES

HOECFoundation is dedicated to compassion, empowerment, inclusivity, collaboration, innovation, sustainability, accountability, and respect. We work to uplift and support individuals and communities with empathy, creativity, and a commitment to making a positive impact.
